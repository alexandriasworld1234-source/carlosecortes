## Major Curriculum and Policy Contributions

1. **UCR Diversity Graduation Requirement** (1970s)
   - First diversity graduation requirement at UCR
   - Precursor to requirements at 1/3 of U.S. colleges

2. **California Textbook Evaluation Task Force** (1971)
   - First statewide task force evaluating ethnic content in textbooks

3. **Suggested Basic Curriculum Principles for Ethnic Studies** (2021)
   - 12-page document for California State Board of Education
   - Helped pass California's Medina Bill (AB 101)
   - California became first state to require high school ethnic studies

4. **City of Riverside Anti-Racism Vision Statement** (2020)
   - 2-page community vision statement
   - Adopted 6-1 by City Council
   - Featured in Los Angeles Times

5. **Health Equity, Social Justice, and Anti-Racism Curriculum** (2020-present)

## The Cortés Triad

Dr. Cortés developed a personal framework to guide his thinking and action:

1. **Look unflinchingly at the past**
2. **Apply it to the present**
3. **Then pivot to the future**

This framework appears throughout his work and teaching.
